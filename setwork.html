'Would You Rather?' - Light Entertainment Set

'Would You Rather?' is a gameshow concept based on the children's game, Would You Rather. 10 contestants face off one another in a game of wits where they are asked a question each round with two alternatives - each with its own challenge. If a contestant fails to complete the challenge given to them or fails to answer, they are knocked out. This project was conceptualised in 2018.

The Music Teacher Play

The Music Teacher is a one-woman show illustrating the life of a music teacher in 50s Manchester. The following is a series of white card model shots and set details to remake the Vaughn Corridor at St. Bede's, Manchester. The architecture is inspired by Florentine palazzos of the early 19th century.

'The Nightingale' by Kristen Hannah, a film adaptation conceptualised in 2019.

The following series of shots arean interioral view of an early '40s apartment in central Paris during the German occupation. The room is sparse but for a table and a few broken chairs being used for firewood. The apartment is dimly lit during the blackout curfew and papers and books scatter the table detailing the paths of the Resistance fighters, specifically the 'passeurs de guerres', French women who smuggled allied men across the French borders.
